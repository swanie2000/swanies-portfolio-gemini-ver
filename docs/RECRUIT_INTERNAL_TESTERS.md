# Recruit internal testers (4 → 12+)

Google needs **at least 12 testers opted in** on the **internal testing** track before you can move toward **closed testing** and production gates. SEO helps long-term; **direct outreach** fills the list faster.

**Canonical link:** `https://swaniedesigns.com/#join-testing`

**Asset:** `website/marketing/facebook-join-testing-post.png`

---

## What testers get (say this clearly)

- **Free** — no Play subscription required during beta
- **90 days Pro** via personal unlock code in your reply email (same Gmail as form + Play + in-app account)
- **Android only** — crypto and/or precious metals portfolio, widgets, local-first vault
- You add their Gmail to **Internal testing → Testers** within ~1 day; they install from Play (not sideload)

**Do not** add recruits to **Settings → License testing** (that list is for accelerated billing QA only).

## Weekly checklist

- [ ] Post in at least **one** new place (FB group, Reddit, etc.)
- [ ] Process **#join-testing** form within 24h
- [ ] Play Console → **Internal testing → Testers** — add Gmail before replying
- [ ] Track count: need **12+** with **opt-in** (installed or accepted invite)
- [ ] After **12**: handoff says promote to **closed testing** (agent can help)

---

## Realistic timeline

- **0 signups from passive SEO** in week one is normal.
- **8 new testers in 2 weeks** is achievable with 3–4 targeted posts + personal network.
- Stuck at 4 for a week usually means **reach**, not product — increase posts and ask each tester to share with one Android friend.
