Press kit

Facts for journalists, reviewers, and testers. Last updated: 2026-05-07

One-liner: Swanie's Portfolio is a local-first Android app for tracking cryptocurrency and precious metals—with vault security, widgets, analytics, and encrypted file backup you control. Automatic Google Drive sync is planned for a future release (not in the current build).

Product

  • Platform: Android (Google Play).
  • Category: Finance / portfolio tracking (crypto & bullion).
  • Monetization: Free core; Pro subscription where available (themes, deeper analytics, widget customization, and related Pro surfaces).
  • Languages: Wide locale coverage; see in-app language list.
  • Privacy policy: Privacy (same site).
