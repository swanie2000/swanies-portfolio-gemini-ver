Private by design.

Your portfolio remains stored on your device by default. Network access is used only for features that require it, such as price data, optional Google Play purchases, or feedback that you choose to send.

Your holdings are not automatically uploaded to cloud storage in this release, and there are no ads in the app. Read the full privacy policy.

Backup when you choose

Export your portfolio as an encrypted .swpb file protected by your own passphrase. Restore through the in-app backup and restore flow when you are ready.

  • Local-first storage
  • Encrypted backup file
  • Optional biometric unlock on supported devices
  • No automatic cloud portfolio upload in this release

Frequently asked questions.

Quick answers about Swanie's Portfolio for Android.

How do I get the app?

Swanie's Portfolio is available on Google Play for Android. Use the Google Play button on this page to open the store listing.

Is the app free?

Yes. The app is free to install. Optional Pro features are available through Google Play Billing where offered.

Can I track gold, silver, and crypto together?

Yes. The app supports cryptocurrency and precious metals in one portfolio, including metals units such as grams, kilograms, and troy ounces.

Where is my portfolio data stored?

Portfolio data is stored locally on your device by default. You can export an encrypted backup file when you want your own backup copy.

Is this investment advice?

No. Swanie's Portfolio is a tracking and organization tool. It is not a broker, bank, or investment adviser. Always verify critical decisions independently.
