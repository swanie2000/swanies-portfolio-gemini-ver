Join testing

Read this page first, then ask to be added. Google Play internal testing only works for accounts the developer adds by hand.

Step 1 — Request access

Internal testing on Google Play does not let random installs. You must use the same Google account email you use in the Play Store. Submit the form below — Swanie will manually add your address to the tester list in Play Console. Until that is done, the tester invite link will not work for you.

Use the exact address you sign into Google Play with.

If the form fails, email with your name, Play account email, and a note that you want internal testing access.

Step 2 — After you are added

  1. Wait for confirmation (or try again later the same day once you know you were added).
  2. Open Open tester invite below (or scan the Play QR). Sign in with the same Google account you requested with.
  3. Accept the Play invitation, then install or update Swanie's Portfolio from the Google Play Store.

About these QR codes & privacy

Site QR encodes this marketing page’s URL so someone at a desk can jump to the same page on mobile without typing. It is not the Play Store install link.

Tester QR encodes your Google Play internal or closed-testing invite URL (the long play.google.com/… link). Scanning it skips retyping that URL on a phone.

Both codes are drawn entirely in your browser using the open-source QRCode.js file shipped in this site (website/js/qrcode.min.js). No third-party “QR image API” is called.
