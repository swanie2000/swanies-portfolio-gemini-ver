Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about the Android portfolio app for crypto and precious metals.

How do I get the app?

Swanie's Portfolio is distributed on Google Play for Android. Use the Get the app section on this page — when the listing is live, the Google Play button will open the store directly.

Is the app free?

Yes — the core app is free to install. Optional Pro features are available through in-app subscription where offered (themes, deeper analytics, widget customization, and related Pro surfaces).

Android only?

Yes. Swanie's Portfolio is an Android app on Google Play. There is no iPhone version.

Where is my portfolio data stored?

Your vault stays on your device (local-first). Encrypted backup is a file you export and keep. Optional cloud sync is planned for a future release; price lookups and optional feedback use the network when you use those features.

Can I track gold, silver, and crypto together?

Yes. The app supports cryptocurrency and precious metals in one portfolio, with gram, kilo, and troy-ounce valuation, widgets, and on-device analytics.

Is this financial advice?

No. Swanie's Portfolio is a tracking tool only. It does not provide investment, tax, or legal advice. See our privacy policy and in-app terms.
