Privacy policy

Effective date: 2026-05-08

1. Who we are

Swanie's Portfolio is developed and published by the Swanie's Portfolio team.

For support questions, use the contact form on this site or the support channel listed on the app's Google Play listing once the app is public.

2. What the app does

Swanie's Portfolio is a portfolio tracker for cryptocurrency and precious metals. Portfolio data is primarily stored on your device. The app may offer optional features that use additional permissions or third-party services only when you choose them.

3. Data stored on your device

The app is designed to store portfolio content primarily on your device, including vault/login data, holdings, portfolio settings, visual preferences, and related app configuration required for normal use.

4. Account and data deletion

Swanie's Portfolio keeps your portfolio and local account information on your device. We do not host a separate cloud copy of your vault for you to delete on our servers in the current release.

Removing some portfolio data (without uninstalling)

You can delete individual holdings and entire vaults from within the app using the portfolio and vault controls (for example in Settings). That removes the selected information from local storage on your device while you keep using Swanie's Portfolio.

How to remove all app data from your device

  • Uninstall the app — removes the app and its local data (including your in-app account and portfolios) from that device.
  • Clear app storage first (optional) — on Android: Settings → Apps → Swanie's Portfolio → Storage, then use Clear storage or Clear data (wording varies by device). That wipes local app data; you can then uninstall if you choose.
  • Factory reset — erases data on the device, including anything stored by the app.

Subscriptions: active Google Play subscriptions are managed in your Google Play account, not on a server we operate. To cancel or change billing, use Google Play’s subscription controls for Swanie's Portfolio (for example from the Play Store app: Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions).

Need help after deletion or for an edge case? Use the support contact listed on the Google Play store listing for Swanie's Portfolio.

5. Optional cloud or network features

  • Google Drive (not in this release): automatic vault sync to Google Drive is planned for a future version; it is not active today. You can still save encrypted backup files wherever you choose (including uploading a file to Drive yourself).
  • In-app purchases / subscriptions (optional): purchase processing is handled by Google Play billing and related subscription infrastructure.
  • Bug reports / feedback (optional): if you submit feedback, the information you provide is used to respond and improve app quality.
  • Market/price data: the app may request market data from third-party providers to calculate and display pricing.

6. Data we do not want or cannot access

We do not sell your personal data. Features are intended to work with minimal data collection and with user-controlled optional services.

7. Children's privacy

Swanie's Portfolio is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us using the support method in the Play listing and we will review and address the request.
