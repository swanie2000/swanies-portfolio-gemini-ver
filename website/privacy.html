Privacy policy

Effective date: 2026-05-08

1. Who we are

Swanie's Portfolio is developed and published by the Swanie's Portfolio team.

For support questions, use the contact method listed in the app's Google Play listing.

2. What the app does

Swanie's Portfolio is a portfolio tracker for cryptocurrency and precious metals. Portfolio data is primarily stored on your device. The app may offer optional features that use additional permissions or third-party services only when you choose them.

3. Data stored on your device

The app is designed to store portfolio content primarily on your device, including vault/login data, holdings, portfolio settings, visual preferences, and related app configuration required for normal use.

4. Optional cloud or network features

  • Google Drive backup (optional): only used when you choose backup/sync features.
  • In-app purchases / subscriptions (optional): purchase processing is handled by Google Play billing and related subscription infrastructure.
  • Bug reports / feedback (optional): if you submit feedback, the information you provide is used to respond and improve app quality.
  • Market/price data: the app may request market data from third-party providers to calculate and display pricing.

5. Data we do not want or cannot access

We do not sell your personal data. Features are intended to work with minimal data collection and with user-controlled optional services.

6. Children's privacy

Swanie's Portfolio is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us using the support method in the Play listing and we will review and address the request.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version is published on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact

Questions: use the support contact listed in Google Play for Swanie's Portfolio.
